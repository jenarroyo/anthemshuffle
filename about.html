Anthem Shuffle provides avid music fans an exciting way to discover new music. Unlike other lyric-generating websites, Anthem Shuffle offers a 'Shuffle' feature that displays a random artist, album, or song. Listeners who would like to share their sentiments, give their own meaning to each lyric, or just express how they feel about a song can speak their own voices. Shuffle on and celebrate your taste in music!

Steps on how to install our Android mobile app.

1. Scan the QR Code (you can download on the app store if you do not have a QR Scanner)
2. You will be redirected to download our app.
3. Download and install the application to your Android device.
4. Enjoy using the AnthemShuffle.
