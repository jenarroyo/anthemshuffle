AnthemShuffle wants to provide its users an exciting new way to discover new lyrics, artists or albums. Unlike other lyrics generating websites, AnthemShuffle offers the Shuffle function which can be used to display a random artist, album or lyrics hence the name AnthemShuffle. Users who would like to comment on posts can do so by logging in using their facebook account.

Steps on how to install our Android mobile app.

1. Scan the QR Code (you can download on the app store if you do not have a QR Scanner)
2. You will be redirected to download our app.
3. Download and install the application to your Android device.
4. Enjoy using the AnthemShuffle.
